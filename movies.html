The Best Movies According to Daniel

My top 3 movies of all-time:

Gladiator

This is my absolute favourite movie because its all about honor, dignity and never giving up.

Forrest gump

Super cool story about a man that experience a whole lot during his life. During the movie you will wonder what's next.

Avatar

Just a really cool sci-fi movie with a great story. Location in the movie is on the planet Pandora, where the human have found a new expensive resource but will the habitant of this planet cooperate and just give this resource away?
